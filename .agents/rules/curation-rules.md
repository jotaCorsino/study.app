# curation rules — complementary materials

## purpose
Complement the local course with useful free online materials, especially from YouTube.

## source logic
The curation must not be random. It should prioritize quality and relevance.

## ranking logic to preserve
- search for videos and playlists related to the course theme
- prefer Portuguese-language relevance when appropriate
- gather channel statistics when available
- prioritize channel authority by subscriber count
- give positive weight to playlists when they fit the topic
- give positive weight to titles containing terms such as:
  - curso
  - completo
  - tutorial
  - aula
  - guia
  - roadmap
  - iniciante
- order candidates by score
- keep the best results associated with the course

## persistence rule
Generated complementary materials must remain saved per course after the app is closed and reopened.
